Our Heritage

Stability you can
bank on.

Meridian Capital was founded in 2008 in Mumbai with a simple idea: serve Indian families and small businesses the way private banks once did, with senior people on the phone and decisions taken by humans, not algorithms. Seventeen years and four lakh customers later, that idea has held up. We are RBI-regulated, CRISIL AA+ rated, and have never missed a payout. Most of our customers came by referral from someone we already serve.
